My Journey

In graphic design

I first got into graphic design back in 2020 during the pandemic.
With plenty of free time and just my laptop, I started creating
profile pictures for people online—sometimes for free, sometimes
for a little something. I joined a bunch of groups, connected
with other creatives, and really enjoyed the process. By 2023, I
took a break for a while, but now I'm back, excited to keep
creating and growing.

In programming

I got into programming in the summer of 2024. Honestly, I was just bored
and stumbled across a video claiming HTML and CSS were the easiest languages ever.
So I thought, why not? After a couple of months of building websites every day,
I pretty much had them down. School slowed me down when it came to JavaScript,
but now I'm back at it and finally diving into it.
